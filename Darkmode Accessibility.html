Accessibility




How to utilize our application.

Our application allows you to claim insurance with Covea with a more updated method of claiming. Our home page will provide you with information about our company, with a menu leading towards your account page, and a page allowing you to make a claim.



Our Claim page allows you to make a new claim, without having to have all that unwanted social interaction. This is key as claiming insurance could be from a very personal matter, so we value your privacy.



If you wish to view your account details you can do this by heading over to our account page. This allows you to view your details and current policies with Covea.
